About

I'm a Senior Email Developer focused on building personalized, data-driven communication experiences that support users throughout their journey. I lead the development of dynamic, responsive email campaigns—leveraging marketing automation platforms to deliver timely and relevant messaging that drives engagement and action. I'm passionate about using technology to solve real problems, and I enjoy mentoring others on best practices that elevate both the message and the experience.
